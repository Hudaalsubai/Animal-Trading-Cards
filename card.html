Blue and Yellow Macaw

"They are popular in aviculture because of their striking color, ability to talk, ready availability in the marketplace, and close bonding to humans."

  • Scientific Name: Ara ararauna
  • Average Length: 76-86 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 30-35 years
  • Habitat:tropical rainforest (South America,Brazil)

The blue-and-yellow macaw also known as the blue- and-gold macaw, parrot with mostly blue top parts and light orange in underparts, with gradient hues of green on top of its head.It is a member of the large group of neotropical parrots k nown as macaws.and weigh 0.9 -1.5 kg.
